Welcome to our FAQ

Are your products natural?


Yes all of our products are 100% all natural and safe to consume!


Do you offer consultations with a nutritionist or health expert?


While we don't provide direct consultations, our staff can offer general advice on supplement selection. For personalized nutrition guidance, we recommend consulting with a healthcare professional!


Do you offer international shipping?


Yes, we offer international shipping. Shipping rates and delivery times may vary based on your location. Please refer to our shipping policy or contact us for specific details!


How do I contact customer support?


You can contact our customer support team through the contact information provided on our website. We are here to assist you with any questions or concerns!


Are your prices competitive?


We strive to offer competitive prices on all our products. Additionally, we may have promotions, discounts, and loyalty programs(coming soon) to provide you with additional savings. Check our website regularly for updates!


What is your policy on expired or damaged products?


We guarantee the quality of our products. If you receive an expired or damaged item, please contact our customer service, and we will arrange a replacement or refund!


Can I request a specific product that is not currently available in your store?


Absolutely! We value your feedback. If there's a specific product you'd like us to carry, please let us know, and we'll explore the possibility of adding it to our inventory!


What is the shelf life of your products?


The shelf life varies by product, but we ensure that all products have a reasonable expiration date. Check the product packaging for this information!


Are your products free from common allergens like gluten and dairy?


Many of our products are labeled to indicate whether they are free from common allergens. Check product descriptions or ask our staff for assistance in finding allergen-free options!


Can I return an opened product if it doesn't work for me?


We understand that everyone's body reacts differently. If you're not satisfied with a product, even if it's been opened, contact our customer service, and we'll work with you to find a solution!


Do you offer a price-match guarantee?


We strive to offer competitive prices, but if you find a lower price for the same product elsewhere, contact us, and we'll do our best to match it!


What steps do you take to minimize environmental impact in your packaging?


We are committed to sustainability. Our packaging is designed with eco-friendly materials, and we actively seek ways to reduce our environmental footprint!


Can I return a product if I experience an allergic reaction?


If you experience an allergic reaction, please discontinue use immediately and contact our customer service. We will guide you through the return process and help you find a suitable alternative!


Do you have a customer satisfaction guarantee?


Yes, we stand behind the quality of our products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer service within a specified period, and we'll work to resolve the issue to your satisfaction!
